My brakes make noise when I engage them. Is this an issue?

If your brakes make a grinding or squeaking noise, it normally means your brake pads are worn out and need to be changed.  It is always best to have any noise your brakes make looked at by a professional technician to make sure it isn't a safely issue.

What is the average life span of a residential air conditions?

Generally, the average life span of any air conditioner is six to seven years. This is subjected to usage pattern and regular servicing of the air conditioner by a qualified air conditioner technician.

What do I do if my hard disk fails to work?

As with most computer errors, your first step is to shut down your computer and restart it. This will help you determine whether or not you actually have a hard disk problem. If the disk is severely damaged then your computer will probably fail to restart properly.

Battery draining issues?

Check whether the battery is drain is caused by any particular app in settings > Battery & performance > Battery usage status or fully charge your device using the supplied charger and check whether battery drain occurs again .If you still can't solve the problem then the device's batteryy is at the end of its life cycle.
